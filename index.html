Splunk Network/Firewall Monitoring Dashboard

I created a custom Splunk dashboard to simulate network traffic analysis. I generated a small sample CSV file with connection events, including source IP, destination IP, and whether the connection was allowed or blocked. After downloading Splunk, and uploading the data into the application, I built a dashboard with three panels showing:

  • Total Connections: 5
  • Allowed Connections: 3
  • Blocked Connections: 2

This hands-on, self learning experience helped me understand how to use Splunk for log analysis and visualization, as well as the basics of SIEM concepts.

Tools: Splunk, Logs, SIEM Concepts
